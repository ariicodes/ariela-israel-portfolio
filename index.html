Meet the Dev

Hello, I’m Ariela! My interest in tech blossomed in 2018 and my love for coding began in 2020. I went from having an interest in DevOps, Cybersecurity, UI/UX Design, then finally Software Engineering. I chose software engineering because feeds my passion for both design and logic. I always like to say that I use both sides of my brain.

My educational background is Cosmetology which I thought was my first love. After working in a salon, I learned that it wasn’t what I desired and I craved more creativity and challenge. During my salon experience, I created marketing materials and email templates which then led me to the design aspect of tech. I then went an extra mile and looked into how the software that I used for design, was created and was immensely intrigued.

I also have professional experience in customer service and retail which also adds to my solutions-based mindset. I took in mind what customers were complaining about when it came to the retailers’ apps and sites as well as the outdated POS system software that didn’t offer what could have made my job easier. During my most recent employment experience, I began following freeCodeCamp’s curriculum and some of Codecademy’s courses and finally believed that I could really make things simpler for others.

Ever since my coding journey began, I’ve become more skilled, dedicated, confident, knowledgeable, and driven. My focus is becoming a MERN Stack Software Engineer and create solutions for all.
